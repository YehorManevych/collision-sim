Controls:

                
Play/Pause - play or pause

                
Step - make another simulation step

                
Rewind - make 40 steps backwards

                
Reset - remove all bodies from the scene and load the specified config again

                
Reset & rewind - reset and rewind

                
t - the size of delta time

                
config - number of a scene to simulate

                
Clear - remove all bodies

                
Show history* - show previous positions of bodies based on their current velocity

                
*Works only when the engine is paused. Also highlights collisions in the past with red

                

                
While paused you can drag the bodies, and change their speed by dragging the red arrow heads
